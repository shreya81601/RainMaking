COME CLOSER TO NATURE

Make your own rain!

Humans can embrace the rain magic through not just listening to it, but trying to imitate the rain! The rain sounds you heard were actually created by us. They're not sounds of real rain falling, but a variety of sounds we collected from our enviornment and combined to give the effect of rain.

Now, we encourage you to be creative! The buttons below represent single sounds we recorded, you are free to use them as you wish and decide how will this rain sound. As you are creating your own rain, try to guess what the following sounds might be - answers will be revealed after a couple of seconds. Let your imagination flow!

What other sounds remind you of rain? Let us know through rain.making@gmail.com!
